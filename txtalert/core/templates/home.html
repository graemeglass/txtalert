USSD Booking tool

While the USSD provider and gateway are still in the process of being setup, the USSD booking tool can in the mean time be tested by using GTalk or any other Jabber/XMPP based chat client by following these instructions:

  • Add vumi.txtalert@praekeltfoundation.org as a contact to your Gtalk contact list.
  • Send your phonenumber as registered in txtAlert as the first chat message. Remember to format your number in full international format without a leading `+` (plus) sign. For 0761234567, which is the patient account's phone number as mentioned above, the correct formatting would be: 27761234567.
  • The menu will return and ask you for your patient id.
  • Send your patient id as the next chat message.
  • Follow the instructions on the screen. From this point on it works like a multiple choice menu.
